Hello, world!

Hi, I'm Jinpeng Li. An Austin-based journalist passionate about using data analytics and visualization to produce Texas news. I'm also pursuing my master's degree in journalism at The University of Texas at Austin.

I love exploring storytelling skills. Before entering the media industry, I won first prize in the New Concept Writing Competition in China for my creative writing and was awarded the Walt Disney Fellowship for my screenwriting.

I'm currently working as a local news reporter for Reporting Texas, previously interned at Yicai Magazine, covering consumer news, and at Caixin Media's data group, reporting on market news and the electric vehicle industry.

Now I'm thirsty opportunity to coborate with code nerd to produce compelling data-driven stories.
